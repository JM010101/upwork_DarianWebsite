DARIAN INTERNATIONAL MISSION STATEMENT

When man colonizes the Moon or Mars, the best minds on our planet will be tasked with designing housing structures which will stand up to any deadly condition the colonizers will face, that is a given.

Yet these same designers and engineers, and the future colonizers with their families back on Earth, and indeed, all the inhabitants on Earth from time immemorial, have never enjoyed the same luxury for some inexplicable reason.

The men of Earth have never chosen to build a habitable structure capable of withstanding Earth's weather and Earths' catastrophic conditions which guarantee their own survival. Witness the most devastating fire in Los Angeles' history in January, 2025. Had anyone up until now cared, such destruction could not happen even with the negligence of governmental officials who ignore their forestry duties or simply insuring there is water in the hydrants.

Darian International is planning to change that historic short sight. We have chosen advanced materials to fabricate a home for the common man, within the common man's budget which will stand up to hurricanes, floods, fires, tornadoes, infestations and to some extent earthquakes.

Tragedies like Hurricane Helene and Milton and Los Angeles could not have happened if men who can design structures to live safely on another planet had done the same for the men, women and children on their own planet Earth.

Darian International has filed patents for a 10" thick concrete home reinforced with steel rebar, and a revolutionary patented waterproof, fireproof, high impact-proof system which will protect every home from water, fire, wind, vermin, high impact, and earth quakes.

Our basic philosophy is built around the childhood nursery rhyme of the three pigs which makes the point if you build your home of sticks, which the majority of Earths structures are and continue to this day to be constructed from, they will quickly be destroyed, or simply rot away.

Additionally, Darian International includes in every home a storm shelter/safe room/bomb shelter/EMP proof room equipped with an emergency power system, oxygen source, food supply and medical supplies which will be as common to every home as a bathroom.

As the current living inhabitants of this planet, we owe it to ourselves, our loved ones and our future generations to carry out this historic shift from stick homes to concrete fortresses which will remain as aesthetically pleasing and beautiful to the eye as any currently constructed home.

We believe firmly we must never forget that we are but visitors to this sometimes uninhabitable and always unpredictable planet and that we are just as vulnerable as the colonizers of the Moon and Mars will be.
